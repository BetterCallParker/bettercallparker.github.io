New NAR Rules Explained Simply

Why Things Are Changing

Following the recent National Association of Realtors (NAR) lawsuit settlement, there are new requirements for buyer broker agreements. These changes are designed to increase transparency in real estate transactions.

What This Means For You:

Written Agreements

You'll now sign a formal agreement before we show you homes. This protects your interests and clearly outlines our responsibilities.

Fee Transparency

All potential costs will be disclosed upfront. You'll know exactly what to expect with no hidden surprises.

Negotiable Fees

Commission rates are fully negotiable, giving you more control over what you pay for services.
